Delete Your MicroGPT Account

This page explains how you can request deletion of your MicroGPT account and the associated data. MicroGPT is published by MeteorMeta (“we,” “us,” or “our”). By following the steps below, you can permanently remove your data from our systems.

1. Data We Remove Upon Deletion

When you request an account deletion, we will remove:

  • Your user identifier (username or name)
  • Your email address and display name
  • Any optional bio information you have provided

Note: If we are required by law or for legitimate business needs (e.g., security or fraud prevention) to retain certain minimal data, we will do so in compliance with applicable regulations but will promptly delete any data no longer necessary.

2. How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your MicroGPT account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to meteormeta.dev@gmail.com.
  2. In the subject line, include: “MicroGPT Account Deletion Request.”
  3. In the body of the email, provide:
    • The email address associated with your MicroGPT account
    • Your randomly generated username, if available
    • Any additional details that can help us confirm your identity (for security)

We will confirm your request within a reasonable timeframe and proceed to delete your account and associated data. Once deleted, your account will no longer be accessible, and the deletion process cannot be reversed.

3. Retention After Deletion

We strive to delete all personal data related to your account promptly once your request has been verified. However, we may retain a minimal amount of data if legally required or for legitimate interests such as:

  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Addressing security or technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations (e.g., records required by law)

Any data retained for these reasons will be stored securely and deleted as soon as it is no longer necessary.
